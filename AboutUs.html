AboutUS

Founded in 2015, UBUNTU Community Outreach (UCO) is a non-profit dedicated to uplifiting communities by providing educational support and career guidance to disadvantaged youth. Ensuring a brighter future for all, making sure each and every youth memeber gets a chance to secure their rightful place in society.

Our Mission

To uplift communities by providing educational support and mentorship for disadvantaged youth

Our Vision

A society where all young people have equal access to education and opportunities.
