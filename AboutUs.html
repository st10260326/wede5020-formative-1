About Us

Founded in 2015, Ubuntu Community Outreach (UCO) is a non-profit dedicated to uplifting communities by providing educational support and career guidance to disadvantaged youth.

Our Mission

To uplift communities by providing educational support and mentorship for disadvantaged youth.

Our Vision

A society where all young people have equal access to education and opportunities.
